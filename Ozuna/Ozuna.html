Ozuna

Ozuna is finally home. Home to him is his wife and children, his music, and his humble beginnings in San Juan, Puerto Rico. Born Juan Carlos Ozuna Rosado on March 13, 1992, he took on his musical persona Ozuna when he chose to pursue his Reggaeton career. His difficult journey has inspired many of his supporters and has gained him many critics along the way.
